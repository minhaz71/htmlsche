Add Your Business to UAE Directory

Want to list your UAE-based business in our directory? We welcome local businesses to showcase their products or services. Please review the terms below before sending your request.

Submission Process

  • Send your business details (Name, Website, Category, Description) to addbusiness@hemdox.com.
  • We review all submissions within 48 hours.
  • Only UAE-based businesses will be accepted.

Terms and Conditions

  • All submitted businesses must have an active website and operate legally in the UAE.
  • We reserve the right to reject any listing that promotes harmful, illegal, or unethical products/services.
  • Business listings are free but subject to approval. Paid promotions are not currently available.
  • We do not guarantee traffic or SEO benefits from listings.
  • Once listed, your business may be removed at any time without prior notice if terms are violated.

For further queries or issues, please contact us at addbusiness@hemdox.com.
